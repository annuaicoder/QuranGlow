About Al-Haqq

Our Mission

Al-Haqq was created with a single purpose: to provide a peaceful, distraction-free space for reading the Holy Quran with proper Tajweed. Every feature — from color-coded Tajweed rules to an ad-free interface — is designed to help readers focus on the words of Allah without interruptions. Whether you’re reciting for study, reflection, or memorization, Al-Haqq offers a clean and calm environment for your journey.
